About ABS Our Story

ABS Group of Schools is a leading educational trust established in 2009. We manage five state-of-the-art campuses designed to offer premium education to students of all backgrounds.

Our schools combine academic rigor with creative development, offering integrated IIT-JEE/NEET coaching, coding & robotics clubs, and rich co-curricular programs including sports, arts, karate, and yoga.
